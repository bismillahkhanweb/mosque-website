Our History & Mission

Established in 1995, Al-Noor Masjid has served as a beacon of light for the local Muslim community. We strive to provide a welcoming environment for worship, education, and community service.

Our mission is to foster an atmosphere of peace, compassion, and understanding, following the true teachings of Islam. We are committed to interfaith dialogue and contributing positively to the wider society.

Imam Yusuf Al-Hassan

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a large parking lot located behind the mosque building with over 100 spaces available for worshippers.

Do you provide facilities for sisters?

Yes, we have a completely separate prayer hall and wudu (ablution) area for sisters, equipped with baby changing facilities.

How can I enroll in Quran classes?

You can register by filling out the contact form below or visiting the administration office after Isha prayers on weekdays.
