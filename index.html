Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements? +

Most beginner courses require only a high school diploma. Advanced courses may require prior knowledge or a portfolio review.

Do you offer job placement? +

Yes, we have a dedicated career services team that assists graduates with resume building, interview prep, and connecting with hiring partners.

Can I pay the fee in installments? +

Absolutely. We offer flexible monthly installment plans for all long-term courses to make education accessible.
